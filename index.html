The Studio

About Sílice Games

Sílice Games is an independent mobile game studio based in Tlaquepaque, Jalisco, México, run by developer Arturo González Rejón.

We craft focused, well-designed Android experiences — games that respect the player's time. Clean interfaces, multiple language support, and thoughtful gameplay are at the core of everything we build.

Every game is made with care, from concept to the Play Store.

Privacy Policy

Sílice Games takes your privacy seriously. Our apps are designed to work offline and collect only the minimum data required to operate.

Data collected: Our apps may use Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Some apps use Firebase (Firestore and Authentication) to sync game progress across devices. Game progress may also be stored locally on your device.

No account required: None of our games require you to create an account or provide personal information to play. In some games, an optional sign-in may be available to back up your progress and restore it across devices — this is always opt-in and never mandatory.

Third-party services: Our apps may integrate Google AdMob and Google Play Billing. Please review Google's privacy policy for details on their data practices.
